Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my order take to arrive?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days within the UK. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days, depending on the destination.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in their original condition with tags attached. Please visit our Returns page for more details.

How do I start a return?

To initiate a return, please log into your account, navigate to your order history, and select the "Return Item" option. Alternatively, contact our customer service team.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

Returns are free for UK customers. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.

How do I determine my size?

Each product page includes a detailed size guide. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

Are your products sustainable?

We're committed to sustainability and ethical manufacturing. Many of our products use organic or recycled materials, and we're continuously working to improve our environmental impact.

Can I wash my Titan Clothing items in the machine?

Care instructions vary by product. Please check the product description or garment tag for specific care recommendations.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never store your full credit card details on our servers.

Will I be charged customs fees?

International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the customer. These fees are not included in your order total.
