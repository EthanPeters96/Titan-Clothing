Returns & Exchanges

Our Return Policy

At Titan Clothing, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in their original condition with tags still attached.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of receipt
  • Products must be unworn, unwashed, and undamaged
  • All original tags and packaging must be intact
  • Sale items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned
  • Undergarments, swimwear, and face masks cannot be returned for hygiene reasons

Exchange Policy

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or color, subject to availability. To request an exchange, please follow the same process as returns and specify your preferred replacement in the notes.

Please Note: Due to high demand, we cannot reserve exchange items until we receive your return. If your preferred replacement is out of stock when we process your return, we'll issue a refund instead.

Return Process

For Online Purchases

  1. Log into your account and navigate to your order history
  2. Select the "Return Item" option for the relevant order
  3. Complete the return form, specifying your reason for return
  4. Print the prepaid return label (UK orders only)
  5. Package the item securely with all original tags
  6. Drop off at your nearest post office or courier collection point

For In-Store Purchases

  1. Visit any Titan Clothing store with your item
  2. Bring your original receipt or order confirmation
  3. Our store associates will assist with your return or exchange
  4. Returns will be refunded to your original payment method
  5. Store purchases without a receipt may be eligible for store credit

Refund Information

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Refund Processing Times:
  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days after processing
  • PayPal: 2-3 business days after processing
  • Store Credit: Immediately available after processing
Return Shipping Costs:
  • UK Customers: Free returns using our prepaid label
  • International Customers: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • Faulty or Incorrect Items: Free returns worldwide (contact customer service first)
